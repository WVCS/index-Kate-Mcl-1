    Cake Man

    The police have identified that there is a "baker's man" running rampant through our little community. Some call him the fastest maker of cake there ever was while others never dare to speak his name. He is identifiable through a single letter placed upon each of his baked goods. People have called in to report sightings, but he appears to be the fastest maker of cakes AND fastest human alive. No one has been able to get a good look at the suspect but one report descibes him as tall, dark, and possibly handsome. Until we understand what this man or, thing is, all we know is to stay inside. Local officals say that the so called "Baker's Man" has a cult followings all over the internet. Online personality and Discord Moderator, PoketortleZ_7, has created a server to keep the stans updated on local sightings and humorous picutres. While interviewing him, the only quote officals could get from PoketortleZ_7 was "hi there...What's your favorite pokemon? Mine is wartortle". He was then seen tipping the end of a white fedora, which was made out to be more horrrifying than the "Baker's Man". Local officals are now advising citizens to be on watch for the "Baker's Man" and "PoketortleZ_7". 🍄 🎂
