    Cake Man

    The police have identified that there is a "baker's man" running rampant through our little community. Some call him the fastest maker of cake there ever was while others never dare to speak his name. He is identifiable through a single letter placed upon each of his baked goods. People have called in to report sightings, but he appears to be the fastest maker of cakes AND fastest human alive. No one has been able to get a good look at the suspect but one report descibes him as tall, dark, and possibly handsome. Until we understand what this man or, thing is, all we know is to stay inside
