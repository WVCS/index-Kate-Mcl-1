Bun's Baked Goods


Our humble little store has been through everything you could imagine! We had a kitchen fire on the first day of our opening and then we had to close due to a nearby danger! We have had many financial hardships during our entire business history, but we manage to scrape by with the love and support from our community. Our establishment has been around since 1983 and we’ve been delivering bun’s baked goods that create smiles, ever since. I hope I can meet you one day and tell you all about our store's rich history!
